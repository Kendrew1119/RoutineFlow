7.  Synopsis : This unit equips students with the knowledge and skills that are required 
to develop Web based applications. The topics covered include setting up 
and configuring Web servers/application servers, technology features for 
handling client requests and generat ing server responses. Further topics 
include technologies for maintaining state in applications, separating 
content from presentation, and connecting and manipulating databases 
and securing a web site. Apart from that, topics covering a range of 
relevant w eb technologies, tool and methods will also be included.
